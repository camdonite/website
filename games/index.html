3D Dots

Play

Remember that silly game that you would play in English class, where you take turns drawing segments in a grid of dots? Being a 3 Dimensional version of the same game, the moves that are available are suddenly much more complex. The "Segments for Cubes" game mode explores the mechanic of winning cubes instead of squares, and the "Planes for Cubes" game mode takes this a step further, where each move is a plane.

Anagrams

Play

Test your knowledge of the English language and try to spell as many words as you can with just a few random letters! You can play alone, or with others, just remember to watch the clock!
